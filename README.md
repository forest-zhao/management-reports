# management-course-reports

In management classes, there are some assignments that require us to select one cutting- edge technology, and design its usage to offer some innovative products or services, design business canvas to conceive the target customers, marketing channels, revenue
sources and calculate the first few years’ revenue, cost and profit. For example, in information technologies and systems 5206, we selected the 3D printed cars which would be flexible to change shape during possible crash and parking;

In understanding IT innovations 5992, we conceived what the impacts about the virtual
reality technology on the medical industry;

In professional practice in IT 5990, we conceived an AI platform for medical X-film, and this platform will give more accurate diagnosis information to patients and reduce their cost and time on medical services, and at the same time make the hospitals get
profit. We also did the survey on the number of hospitals in Australia, the renting cost, the salary of AI technicians and etc to calculate the profits to run the AI platform. And make the Gantt chart to allocate people and cost to different tasks, analysis and make
plans for possible risks and etc;

In project management 6007, we conducted management plans for a concrete project from dimensions like cost, scope, communication, quality, risk and etc. 

In advanced project management 6010, we are given the company’s departments situations, their current products and services, their established IT system, and how the third party IT company work for this company, their statements about communication
barriers, and the company’s location distribution, their products’ market share and annual growth rate, and their company’s vision, CEO’ attitude and department heads’ attitude toward new information systems. We finally gave advice on the future IT solutions and road map to make CEO and department heads know the benefit of IT and convince them to accept our suggestions
